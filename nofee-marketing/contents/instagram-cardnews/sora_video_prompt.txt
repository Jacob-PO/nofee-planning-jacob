---

**VISUAL PROMPT (ENGLISH):**

A heartwarming 15-second cinematic story shot in Korean home setting.

SCENE 1 (0-3s): Close-up of Korean mother in her 60s sitting on a living room couch, looking worried and skeptical while looking at her adult daughter, soft natural window light, warm beige interior, shallow depth of field, concern visible on her face.

SCENE 2 (3-6s): Over-the-shoulder shot of Korean daughter in her 30s showing smartphone screen to her mother, both sitting close together on couch, warm indoor lighting, intimate moment, focus on the phone screen displaying review interface with Korean text and 5-star ratings.

SCENE 3 (6-9s): Close-up of mother's face gradually changing expression from skeptical to relieved and surprised, eyes widening slightly, gentle smile forming, emotional transformation captured beautifully, soft focus background.

SCENE 4 (9-12s): Medium shot of mother and daughter walking together happily through a bright modern Korean shopping district during daytime, natural smiles, casual comfortable clothing, slight motion blur suggesting movement, warm atmosphere.

SCENE 5 (12-15s): Final shot pulls back to reveal the two walking away into the bustling street, warm golden hour lighting, slice of life moment, authentic street photography style, peaceful and hopeful ending.

Cinematography: Handheld camera with subtle movements, warm color palette with cream and beige tones, soft natural lighting throughout, photorealistic style, emotional depth, intimate family storytelling, Korean cultural context.
